Using Taboo

To use Taboo, just click on this icon, found next to your address bar. This saves the tab for you.
Saved Taboos display this icon instead. Clicking this again will the taboo again, updating the screenshot and saved state. (shift-clicking this will move the taboo to the trashcan)
To view your saved taboos, click on this icon.
